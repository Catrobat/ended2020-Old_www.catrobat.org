Show it to the world

Of course you want to show your work to others. You can upload your project, no mater if it is a newly created one or a remix of another one. To upload your work you have to go trough the following steps:

Step 1: In Pocket Code click on 'Upload'. A 'Login' window will appear. Choose a username and a password, and after that click 'Login or Register'.

Step 2: After logging in successfully an 'Upload program' dialog will appear. Now give your project a meaningful name and description. Then click 'Upload' - done!

Step 3: After uploading your project, you and others can find it by either clicking 'Explore' in the app menu or by visiting the website www.pocketcode.org
