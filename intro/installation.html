Installation of Pocket Code

There are two ways to install Pocket Code:

  • From a PC
  • From your Smartphone

Step 1: If you are in front of your PC right now, click on the following link: Let's go.

Step 2: Click on 'Install'. If you are not logged in, you will be asked to sign in with your Google account. (Hint: Use the same account you use for your smartphone or tablet)

Step 3: The app will now be installed on your smartphone automatically. (Hint: Your smartphone needs to be connected to the internet in order to install the app)

Step 1: Open the playstore-app on your smartphone.

Step 2: Enter 'PocketCode' in the search bar and choose the first result. Then, click 'Install'.

Step 3: Accept the app permissions. Pocket Code will now be installed.
