How can I participate with my school?

We would like to give you the chance to take part in this unique event for free and win great prizes for all participating students as well as the class teacher!

As the class teacher, please follow these steps:

  • Register your class by filling out the registration form.
  • During registration, you can choose whether you want to receive free Galaxy Game Jam posters from us.
  • After filling out the registration, you will receive a notification with a unique class ID by email.
  • Your students can create their programs individually or in teams of up to three students. In order to be eligible for the special prizes available for teachers in selected countries, at least 10 students of your class must participate. Please note the general rules on the home page.
  • The finished games must be submitted by your students as described on the home page.
  • Important: During the submission process, students must select the "school class" option in the registration form.
  • In the next step "Class ID" students must enter the ID that you will receive by E-mail after registering your class.
  • All winners will be notified after the end of the game jam in January 2017 by E-Mail.



Additionally, we prepared some workshop and lesson materials for you and your class. To check them out please visit our edu platform!


If you have any questions, please contact us via e-mail. We would be pleased to answer your questions!

Good luck with the participation in the Galaxy Game Jam!
