Privacy

The collected data from the project submission and the group participation will get used and stored by the mentioned association to organise the Galaxy Game Jam, which includes the notification of winners and shipping/sending of information material about the Galaxy Game Jam, during and shortly after the event. You can withdraw this data use by contacting us via E-Mail or mail (see details above) at any time and without any statement of reasons. However, due to the missing data, participating at the Game Jam would not be possible any longer.
