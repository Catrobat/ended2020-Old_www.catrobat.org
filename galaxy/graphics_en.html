Graphics for your game

Characters

Meet our little Space-Adventurers we created for you to use in your games. You are invited to change and adapt them any way you want.

Have fun discovering your very own galaxy together with them!

You will find all graphical assets in our Pocket Code Media Library. When you add a new object or a new look for an object, you can select them via the "Media Library" button (second from left after tapping on "+").

Backgrounds

We also created some backgrounds for your games. Of course you can change them in any way you want.

To get them, simply visit our Pocket Code Media Library. When you add a new background image, you can select them via the "Media Library" button (second from left after tapping on "+").
